About Me

Physicist, risk quant, and just a guy from the islands. Deeply interested in understanding network structure as a causal factor in the dynamics of financial markets. I believe understanding and visualizing this structure is necessary to manage market, credit and liquidity risk. Broadly speaking, I want to apply this to the defensive side of trading and asset management.

Otherwise open to pretty much any interesting problem, always looking for new insights, and novel takes on the world.
